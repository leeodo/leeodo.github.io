Executive Summary

 

Reddit is an American social news aggregation, content rating, and discussion website. As of March 2022, Reddit ranks as the 9th-most-visited website in the world and 6th most-visited website in the U.S., according to Semrush. Registered users submit content to the site such as links, text posts, images, and videos, which are then voted up or down by other members. Posts are organized by subject into user-created boards called “communities” or “subreddits”. Within the time range from January 1, 2021 to August 31, 2022, the top subreddits are shown below. ‘AskReddit’, ‘FreeKarma4U’ and ‘GaySnapchat’ are the top 3 subreddits with the most submission numbers.

Analysis Report

 

 

We use the reddit submission and reddit comments data from January 1, 2021 to August 31, 2022. Comments dataset has 4,473,556,762 rows and 21 columns. Submission dataset has 643,255,528 rows and 68 columns. The datasets are huge, so we configured a Microsoft Azure cluster with 8 workers, 112 GB memory and 32 cores to process data. After checking the definitions of each column, we proposed 10 business questions.

To answer the KOL’s usage preference within a day, we subset the comments dataset by filtering out the top 100 authors with the most ‘gilded’. Gilding is an award system in Reddit to show appreciation on helpful comments. We believe that the authors with the most gilded generate the better-quality comments and can be called KOLs. After getting the top 100 KOL subset, we converted the UTC format time to year, month, day and hours. Grouping by hours, we get the usage preference within a day.

 

Figure 3 - Most Popular Reddits by Submission+Comments

 

 

Switching gear to subreddit level, we get the top 10 subreddits with in the time range. Top three are ‘AskReddit’, ‘FreeKarma4U’ and ‘GaySnapchat’. After mixing number of comments and number of submission, we get the new top subreddits. To dive deep in the hottest subreddit- ‘AskReddit’, we want to see if people were discussing the things the affected their day-to day life. We created a column called ‘question_topic’ by searching keywords of the breaking news such as Ukraine, Gas Price, Crypto. Then we get the number of submissions in ‘AskReddit’ that related to these topics.

Besides these two reddit datasets themselves, we imported external data of the car sales in the U.S. Here we choose BMW to make a plot. Using regular expression to created dummy variables of whether a comment is related to BMW, we created a plot with two Y- Axis to see if there is correlation between comments related to BMW and BMW sales by month. It seems like there is no obvious relationship between the number of comments and the sales of BMW. We guess one reason may be car sales is highly seasonal, which is related to the marketing strategy and their discount. We guess the second reason may be that there are less than 50% user are from the U.S, so the number of comments in Reddit may be fully reflect the BMW sales only in the U.S.
